About Yuma Hearth

Founded in 2022, Yuma Hearth is the culmination of generations of bakers who poured their love for bread into every loaf. Our family lineage traces back to Old World recipes passed down and perfected right here in Yuma, AZ. We believe in using locally sourced grains and ingredients, supporting nearby farmers and artisans who share our passion for quality.

Each morning our ovens come alive with the aromas of scratch-made sourdoughs, baguettes, and specialty breads. From neighborhood markets to community events, we strive to be more than a bakery—we’re a gathering place for friends and family. When you enjoy our bread, you’re sharing in the spirit of Yuma.
